How it works

Step one

The kilostore works a bit differently than
most stores. When you find
an item you like you
first check what
color tag
it has

Step two

When you know what color the item
belongs to you go over to one of the
scales in the store, remove the cloathing
hanger if it has one and put the item
on the scale. You then press the
button with the color of the item
and see how much it costs.
