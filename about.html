The kilo store is a vintage store located on
waterlooplein in amsterdam. The store mostly sells clothing
from the 70's but also has a lot of clothing from the
60's, 80's and other eras. The store is opend on
Monday-Sunday 11:00-19:00 and Sunday 11:00-18:00

For more information please visit the kilostore website

Why should you shop at the kilo store?

There are defenetly very good reasons to
shop at the kilo store!

In my opinion the most important is the
enviorment. By shopping at the kilostore
you are buying secondhand vinage clothing
and are therefor not contributing to the
production of new clothing. This for
many people is the first step to
a greener life.
